Fish Recipe

Description

This Lemon Garlic Butter Baked Tilapia is a quick, healthy, and flavorful weeknight dinner that requires minimal cleanup. The mild tilapia fillets are bathed in a rich sauce of butter, fresh lemon, and garlic, creating a delicate, flaky texture that tastes gourmet but is ready in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Tilapia Fillets: 4 (approx. 1.5 lbs)
  • Butter: 4 tablespoons, melted (unsalted preferred)
  • Lemon: 1 fresh lemon, divided into 2 tbsp juice and some zest
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Seasonings: 1/2 tsp dried thyme, 1/2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Garnish: Chopped fresh parsley, extra lemon slices

Steps

  1. Preparation: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease or line a baking dish. Rinse and pat dry the tilapia fillets before placing them in the dish.
  2. Sauce and Baking: Combine melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, zest, and seasonings in a bowl. Pour this sauce over the fish and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the tilapia is opaque and flakes easily.
  3. Finish: Garnish with parsley and lemon slices and serve immediately, optionally spooning the pan drippings over the fish. You can also broil for the last 1–2 minutes for a crispier top.
